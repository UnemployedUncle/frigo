FRIGO

Slow-Roasted Heirloom Tomato
& Garlic Confit Risotto

Prep Time 45 Minutes
Difficulty Intermediate
Servings 4 Persons

01 / Ingredients

Inventory Check: Optimal

Pantry & Fresh

Arborio rice 2 cups
Heirloom tomatoes 1.5 lb
Garlic 1 head
Vegetable stock 6 cups
Parmigiano Reggiano To taste

Shopping List

3 missing
  • Dry white wine (Sauvignon Blanc)
  • Fresh Basil (1 bunch)
  • Arborio rice (refill)

02 / Cooking Process

Step-by-Step Guidance
01

Confit the Garlic & Tomatoes

Halve the heirloom tomatoes and peel garlic cloves. Submerge in olive oil with fresh thyme and slow-roast at 300°F (150°C) until jammy and fragrant.

Duration 45 Minutes
Temp 300°F
02

Prepare the Stock Base

In a separate pot, bring vegetable stock to a low simmer. Maintaining a consistent temperature is key to achieving that signature creamy risotto texture.

03

Toast & Deglaze

Sauté finely minced shallots in butter until translucent. Add Arborio rice and toast for 2 minutes. Deglaze with dry white wine, stirring until the liquid is fully absorbed.

04

The Gradual Stir

Add warm stock one ladle at a time, stirring constantly. Wait until each ladle is absorbed before adding the next. Finish by folding in the tomato confit and freshly grated parmesan.

Service concludes with presentation.

Ensure plated elements meet the specified temperature standards. Garnish with fresh basil micro-greens for aromatic elevation.
